Why Your Donation Matters

One Shot Leadership serves Native American youth in one of Montana's most under-resourced rural communities. Your donation provides:

  • Mentorship and adult role models for youth aged 10–18
  • Leadership camps and retreats
  • Crow Nation cultural heritage programming
  • Academic tutoring and college preparation
  • Community gathering events
