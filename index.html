Our Mission

To equip Native American youth in rural Montana with leadership skills, mentorship, and cultural grounding that create lasting positive change.

🌟

Our Vision

A future where every Native youth stands confident in who they are, equipped to lead their families and communities forward.

🤝

Our Values

Respect, resilience, community accountability, and a deep honoring of Crow Nation heritage and Indigenous traditions.

Who We Are

Rooted in Wyola, MT — Committed to Native Youth

One Shot Leadership was founded in Wyola, Montana, on the Crow Indian Reservation, by Joshua Stewart and a group of community members who saw a critical need: young people who lacked consistent mentors and structured pathways to leadership.

We believe every young person deserves exactly one clear shot at a better future — and our job is to make sure that shot counts.
